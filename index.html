Features

to ride smarter

One subscription unlocks all transit modes, real-time tracking, and smart fare management across the city.

Live Route Tracking
Track your bus or matatu in real-time on a live map. Know exactly when your ride arrives — no more waiting blind.

Smart Subscriptions
Choose daily, weekly, or monthly plans. Unlimited trips, pre-loaded passes, or route-specific packages — all in one app.
